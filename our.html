Clinical Quality & Outcomes:

Quality could mean different things to different people. But, for us at Parivar Super Speciality Hospitals & Research Centre, Gwalior, what is most paramount is the quality of clinical care given to each patient who is treated by us.

High-quality, evidence-based care leads to saving more lives and less time in the hospital. Quality care also means safer care for patients. This involves constantly and proactively looking at processes to improve safety and, in turn, quality.


Why is this important to know?

Quality is measured in many ways. There are Process Indicators (those that measure parameters such as timeliness and baseline practices) and Outcome Indicators (such as infection rates and complication rates).


We, at Parivar Super Speciality Hospital & Research Centre, Gwalior, pursue clinical quality through many initiatives.
  1. Measuring Outcomes
  2. Patient Safety
  3. Infection Control
  4. PSH Standards of Clinical Care.
  5. IT Excellence
